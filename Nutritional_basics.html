Key Principle: A balanced diet includes a variety of foods from all food groups in the right proportions.

Essential Nutrients

  • Carbohydrates: Your body's main energy source. Choose whole grains, fruits, and vegetables over refined sugars.
  • Proteins: Essential for building and repairing tissues. Include lean meats, fish, beans, and legumes.
  • Fats: Necessary for hormone production and nutrient absorption. Focus on healthy fats from nuts, avocados, and olive oil.
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Support immune function and overall health. Eat a colorful variety of fruits and vegetables.
  • Fiber: Aids digestion and keeps you full. Found in whole grains, fruits, vegetables, and legumes.
