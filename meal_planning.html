Pro Tip: Planning your meals in advance helps you make healthier choices and saves time and money!

Weekly Meal Planning Steps

  • Set aside planning time: Dedicate 30 minutes each week to plan your meals.
  • Check your schedule: Plan simpler meals for busy days.
  • Use the plate method: Fill half your plate with vegetables, one quarter with protein, and one quarter with whole grains.
  • Prep ingredients: Wash and chop vegetables, cook grains in bulk, and marinate proteins ahead of time.
  • Make a shopping list: Stick to your list to avoid impulse purchases.
  • Embrace leftovers: Cook larger portions to have healthy meals ready for the next day.
