Remember: While superfoods are nutritious, variety is key. No single food can provide all the nutrients you need!

Top Superfoods to Include

  • Berries: Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber. Great for heart and brain health.
  • Leafy Greens: Spinach, kale, and Swiss chard are rich in vitamins A, C, K, and minerals.
  • Salmon: High in omega-3 fatty acids that support heart and brain health.
  • Nuts & Seeds: Almonds, chia seeds, and flaxseeds provide healthy fats and protein.
  • Quinoa: A complete protein source with all essential amino acids.
  • Sweet Potatoes: Rich in beta-carotene, fiber, and vitamins.
  • Greek Yogurt: High in protein and probiotics for gut health.
  • Green Tea: Contains antioxidants and may boost metabolism.
